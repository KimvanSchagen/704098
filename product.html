Smart swimming goggles and how to use them:

Smart swim goggles are the worlds first augmented-reality swim goggles. It combines latest wearable-tech and data analytics to make a virtual coach for the athletes. These ordinary looking swimming goggles feature a series of sensors that record the wearer’s performance as well as feature a display on the lens. Upon purchase, a professional swimmer can download the corresponding app and sync it with their smart goggles. The goggles virtual display will also show the athlete a timer, their speed, and other various stats of their choosing. The applications smart algorithm stores a large amount of information specifically developed to generate proper advice for the swimmer, based on their personal goals.

What do the smart goggles measure?

The smart goggles measure various different statistics. These stats include the laps, time, distance, heartrate, split times, stroke rate, stroke count, distance per stroke, calories burned and so on. They essentially measure the entirety of the swim broken down into every possible statistic which can be measured using their developed algorithm. Based on the personal goals of the athlete, one specific skill can be targeted to be improved. For example, the heart rate and distance per stroke is a very significant metric for a triathlon athlete whose goal is to conserve their energy as much as possible.

What is the future of smart goggles?

Technological advances have hardly been easy to predict but one can expect that high-tech wearable technologies are here to stay in the sporting industry. One can already use smart swimming goggles for personal use, but all trends point towards them becoming a staple in every swimming athlete training regimen. These goggles will also be able to measure the likeliness to get injury as well generate newer techniques using AI in the future. Also using virtual reality, athletes should eventually be able to recreate their experience of swimming without having to do so with a possibility of sharing it, ultimately allowing for an unprecedented rate of collaboration.

How smart goggles are changing the competitive swimming industry:

Innovations like the smart swimming goggles are turning sports into a calculable science. Because of the high degree of efficiency these goggles can generate, coaches could rather use the goggles to monitor their athletes’ performance. A competitive swimmers entire swim can be monitored in great detail to be analyzed by the coach and can be used to build strategies. Smart goggles are allowing athletes to tackle the problems which would not be otherwise possible due to human-limitations.

A swimmer’s personal experience with smart goggles:

For professional swimmers, smart goggles are a great tool to monitor and improve their performance. Many athletes like competitive triathlete Collin Sherrick were asked to share their personal experience with the product. “Having data on the screen has helped me make progress in real time” Colin said when expressing his delight with the product. He claimed to have improved his stride and speed in the 50 and 100m relays and heavily advocates the product to his cohorts. Many athletes like him have testified to seeing measurably better results after using the product.

Are smart goggles worth the money?

Smart goggles feature latest wearable technology with waterproof sensors and a smudge free display. It stands to reason that this device is not cheap. A pair of these goggles will set you back anywhere between 200 to 500 euros depending on their build quality and features as well as a subscription fee for the app that will analyses the athlete’s data. This poses the question if they are worth the money. Many athletes have testified to seeing results but unless you are a professional competitive swimmer, one can argue that this level of tech is not necessary for you especially if you lack the basic understanding of how to apply the generated analysis from the app.
